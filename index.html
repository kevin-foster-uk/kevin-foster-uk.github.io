About Me

I'm a freelance software developer based in Liverpool, England, with over 20 years of experience delivering bespoke software solutions. I specialise in building custom applications tailored to meet specific business needs.

My work is driven by a strong focus on quality, clarity, and long-term maintainability. I take pride in writing clean, well-structured code and applying proven software engineering principles to every project.

Over the years, I've come to deeply value the strength of effective team collaboration. I've learned that the overall quality and cohesion of a team far outweigh the brilliance of any individual contributor. A high-trust environment—where team members feel supported by each other and by management—is essential for building motivated, focused, and productive teams. This belief strongly influences how I approach both solo and collaborative projects.

Outside of client work, I'm constantly exploring new technologies and refining my development practices. I have a particular interest in object-oriented programming and methodologies that help improve software quality and reliability.
